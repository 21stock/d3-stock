d3-Stock

A Stock price visualization using d3.js.

See, all in one chart, with multiple time spans (1wk, 1mth, 6mths, 1yr, 5yr):

  • actual stock prices
  • a mobile average
  • trading volumes

Example: AAPL Stock price
